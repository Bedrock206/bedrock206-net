FIGHTING_SPIRIT

Take on the world. Burn through them all.

"If you want to die, step up!" -Kazuma Kiryu

Fighting Spirit is your guide to the worlds where hostility is the solution. That hostility will let you take a beating, lash back at an insult, and keep the fire in you going.

At high levels, Fighting Spirit becomes the only solution you have. At low levels, Fighting Spirit will let you be stepped on and kicked aside without complaint.

STEAM_ENGINE

Lock in. Tune out.

"Burned... It's all burned away... Burnt to white ashes..." -Ashina No Joe, Ch. 111

Steam Engine allows you to become the true mechanical self, keeping you going as long as there's breath in your lungs and a beat in your heart. It will gladly take control of your body and allow the mind to wander.

At high levels, Steam Engine will cause you to ignore important bodily signals, potentially to your undoing. On the other hand, too weak of a Steam Engine will have you stumble upon every discomfort.
