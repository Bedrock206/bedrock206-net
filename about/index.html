ABOUT MY SITE

this website serves as a bit of an externalization of myself, with as much honesty as i can muster. my friends know that i often journal publically in our discord servers, but this is intended to be another medium fur that sort of thing.

content on this website is generally written once without much revision, not spellchecked, and probably of low overall quality. i do this because i don't particularly care about consistency in that way and find that writing while worrying about the audience kinda stifles me. i apologize if anything isnt accessible, i do my best to ensure that voice readers and whatnot can navigate okay.

i hope that this website exists in some form fur a long time. someday i'll have a nice homelab setup to ensure that.
